Task 8: Love reminders

Tip: TypeScript OR type: https://www.webdevtutor.net/blog/typescript-type-or-and

Oh no! I am a forgetful and anxious person in love. I need to be reminded and reassured of your love.
Create a function that takes in one parameter of any type, depending on the parameter,
return different statements of love.
You can choose what to check on and what statements to return.
The function should accept at least numbers, strings and booleans.

Reference examples:
    - 3 => I love you
    - false => I'll always love you
    - I am insecure => I got you, bebbehh
    - 2433 => Forever is not enough time to be with you
