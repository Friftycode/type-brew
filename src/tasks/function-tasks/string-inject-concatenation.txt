Task 2: String inject concatenation

Create one single function.
Takes in two parameters that are text.
Prints out one single text connecting them in this order: second input, the word "love" , first input.

Examples of inputs and outputs:
1. 'I am great', 'You are great' => 'You are great love I am great'
2. 'Monday', 'Saturday' => 'Saturday love Monday'
3. 'Sweetie', 'Darling' => 'Darling love Sweetie'
4. 'Sugar', 'Cake' => 'Cake love Sugar'
5. 'I am first', 'No, I am first' => 'No, I am first love I am first'
